About FriendJournal

At FriendJournal, we believe that memories shared with friends are some of the most cherished moments in life.FriendJournal helps you keep those memories alive, together with friends.

Our History

Founded in 2024, FriendJournal started with the idea of helping people document their friendships in a meaningful way. FriendJournal is a trusted space for friends to reflect, share, and grow together.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a safe, creative space where people can capture and share the moments that matter most. With a focus on security, privacy, and ease of use.
